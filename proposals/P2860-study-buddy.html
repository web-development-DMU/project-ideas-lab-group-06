StudyBuddy Proposal

Application Name:

StudyBuddy

Target Users:

  • University students looking for study partners or group sessions
  • Course instructors wanting to suggest study groups

User Requirements:

  • Students can create and join study groups
  • Students can schedule sessions with date/time and location
  • Users can post resources, notes, and discussion topics
  • Instructors can moderate or suggest groups for specific courses

Why Users Need It:

Students often struggle to find effective study partners. StudyBuddy helps them collaborate, share resources, and plan sessions efficiently, improving learning outcomes.

Database Tables (4 main tables):

  • Users: student_id, name, email, password, role (student/instructor)
  • Groups: group_id, name, course, description
  • Sessions: session_id, group_id, date, time, location
  • Resources: resource_id, session_id, title, link/file
