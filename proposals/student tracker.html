MY APP

by sinayomi kolawole

I want to make a Student Budget and Expense Tracker which could be a clean website where users can track spending, this website would allow students to:

  • Add income (e.g., student loan, job)
  • Add expenses (food, rent, transport)
  • View spending by category
  • See a simple chart of where money goes
  • Add expenses (food, rent, transport)

a short explanation of what this app would be able to do is track your spending efficently and help budget your money. You will be able to view your sending by catagory and also set limits on how much you plan to spend on certain catgories such as , transportation , food , and rent. You will also be able to see your total planned income for the month to help u clearly see your money and use it wisely.

Types of users this app will have

this app will have:

  • Regular users - students who will use the app to help them with their budgeting and spending.
  • admin users - people who maintain and manage the app (me)
  • non users - people who dont have an account and are yet to sign up
  • premium users who pay to have extra features in the app.
