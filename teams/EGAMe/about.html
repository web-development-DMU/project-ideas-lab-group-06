About EGAMe

Your go-to destination for discovering and purchasing the best games at great prices.

Who We Are

EGAMe is a team of three passionate Computer Science students studying at De Montfort University. United by our love for gaming and technology, we set out to build an ecommerce platform that makes buying games simple, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone.

Our name stands for our shared enthusiasm — we live and breathe games, and we want to bring that energy to every customer who visits our store.

What We Do

We offer a curated selection of games spanning every genre — from action-packed adventures and immersive RPGs to competitive multiplayer titles and relaxing indie experiences. Whether you are a casual player or a hardcore gamer, EGAMe has something for you.

Our platform lets you browse, add games to your basket, and check out with ease. We have built a straightforward shopping experience so you can spend less time navigating menus and more time playing.

Our Mission

We believe that great games should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide a trustworthy and user-friendly platform where gamers can find their next favourite title without breaking the bank.

As students ourselves, we understand the value of a good deal. That is why we are committed to offering competitive prices and a hassle-free experience from start to finish.

Why Choose EGAMe?

Unlike larger retailers, we are a small team that genuinely cares about every customer. We handpick our catalogue to ensure quality, and we are always open to feedback. When you shop with EGAMe, you are supporting a student-led project built with real passion and dedication.
