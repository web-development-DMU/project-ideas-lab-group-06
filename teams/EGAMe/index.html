What is EGAMe?

EGAMe is an ecommerce platform built by three Computer Science students at De Montfort University. We sell a wide variety of games — from the latest console titles to classic board games — all in one place.

Create an account, browse our catalogue, add games to your basket, and purchase them hassle-free. Whether you are after a new PS5 release or a family board game night, we have got you covered.

Why Shop With Us?

We are gamers ourselves, so we know what matters — great selection, fair prices, and a smooth shopping experience. Our catalogue is handpicked to ensure quality across every genre and platform.

As a student-led project, we keep things simple and honest. No hidden fees, no fuss — just great games delivered to you.
